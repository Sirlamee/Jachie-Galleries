Silence

This piece "SILENCE " is inspired by the emotional patterns of everyday life. Psychologists say humans experience over 27 distinct emotions, but most of them are rarely expressed openly. In Nigeria especially, people are taught to "be strong," so much of what we feel is hidden behind silence, laughter, or routine. I chose to use discarded fabrics because fabric in Africa is not just cloth it carries memory, culture, and identity. By using thrown-away fabric, I show how even what is forgotten can become beautiful again. To enquire or purchase, please contact me via email
